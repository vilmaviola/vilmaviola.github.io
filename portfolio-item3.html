Pocémotchi

Mobile App Development

A mobile app called Pokémotchi. The app is a combination between Pokémon and Tamaguchi so the idea is that you take care of your Pokémon and battle with your Pokémon against other users. We used React Native and Expo to develop this app.

My role in this project was to handle the data saving and the data fetching from our storage. I implemented this by using Async storage. I implemented Async Storage for the Pokémon’s ID, for unique user IDs to allow differentiation and also for the data about hunger, fun and cleanliness. I was also in charge of fetching the data from Async storage whenever needed. So, for login, for new Pokémon’s, for battles, etc.


The only problems I encountered had to do with the time consumption it took to save and load data. This was solved by using promises so that the data is fetched from the storage before moving on to other things. When the Pokémon status and data about hunger and such reaches a number that is divisible by ten the data is saved in the storage, and also once right in the beginning of the Pokémon's life.
