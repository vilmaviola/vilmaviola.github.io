About me

A creative and organized engineer student

I am a creative, driven and organized engineer student with skills and competence within programming, design and problem solving.
Media Technology is a technical and scientific engineering education with focus on interaction design, front-end development and user experience (UX). I am seeking a career where I can try to impact the way that people experience technology.

I enjoy frontend development and UX design a lot. To analyse data and turning seemingly endless amounts of data into a structured, visually appealing and understandable representation of user behaviour is also something that I find interesting.

From work experience I realized that I really enjoy working with quality assurance because it truly speaks to my inner perfectionist. Concept development and product managing was also something that I through work experience found was a great fit for me.

Finally, I am a curious person who likes diversified assignments and who is eager to learn. I am excited to start working and using all my acquired knowledge and try to impact the way that people experience technology.
